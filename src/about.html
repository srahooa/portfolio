A lil' bit about me

Communication: Without which, nothing would get done.

Compassion: Treating everybody in the same manner which I wish to be treated.

Collaboration: Bringing together multiple perspectives to produce the best results.

I started my journey into tech and web development after a recent move to Australia. It was a moment when I could invest in myself, up-skill, and embark on a career change.

Previously, I worked in the arts; an industry that supported my passion for learning. My work as an archivist introduced me to the world of data and immediately piqued my interest. Ever since I have been keen to learn more about data, harnessing it, and the tools that enable us to do so. Yet, I still maintain my interest in the arts; translating into a fascination with UX/UI.

My keen interest in travel and cultures has taken me across the globe. I have studied and worked in the United States, Turkey, the UK, the Republic of Georgia, and now Australia. This global experience brings a unique perspective which I apply to my work.

When I am not improving my ability to code I enjoy good food, delving into a good book, or kitesurfing.
